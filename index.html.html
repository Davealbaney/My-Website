My Story

Built in the fire. Defined by the people.

I studied Business & Marketing at Oxford Brookes University before stepping onto one of London's most demanding graduate sales floors — and staying for a decade. From day one, I was immersed in the real world of high-performance sales: fast-paced, high-pressure and unforgiving.

Those years were spent in the fire. Fast-paced, high-pressure, relentless. But rather than simply surviving, I thrived — and more importantly, I learned how to help others thrive. From the very beginning, I was drawn to developing the people around me.

Over 17 years I have personally coached, developed and inspired over 500 individuals — from entry-level graduates to senior directors. What I am most proud of is not a revenue figure. It is my retention rate. In industries notorious for burnout and churn, my teams stayed, grew and won — because they felt genuinely respected and invested in.
